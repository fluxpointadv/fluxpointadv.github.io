  We model the connected behaviour of atmosphere, ocean, sea ice, and tides to deliver integrated projections and decision-relevant outputs across sectors and geographies.

  ### What we help you answer

  - What climate and ocean conditions should I plan for over the next 20–50 years?
  - How will sea level rise, tidal dynamics, and storm surge combine to affect my coastline or asset?
  - How will sea ice loss and ocean circulation shifts affect my region or operations?
  - How do physical climate risks translate into operational or financial exposure?

  ### Includes

  - Downscaled climate projection and scenario analysis
  - Ocean heat content, circulation, and sea surface temperature modelling
  - Sea ice extent, variability, and decline projections
  - Tidal constituent analysis and coastal hydrodynamic modelling
  - Storm surge, compound flood, and extreme sea level assessment
  - Physical climate risk translation for assets and operations
  - Pathway modelling under IPCC and custom emissions scenarios
