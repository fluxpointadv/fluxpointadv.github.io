About

Fluxpoint Advisory is a science-led consultancy founded by two researchers with PhDs from the University of Tokyo. We bring experience from academia, industry, and operational environmental systems, combining scientific rigour with practical application.

Our expertise spans environmental systems analysis, life cycle assessment, carbon accounting, and climate and ocean modelling, supporting evidence-based decision-making in sustainability, decarbonisation, and climate resilience.

Through international research collaborations and consultancy projects, we have supported organisations across Asia, Europe, Africa, and North America, helping clients navigate complex environmental and climate challenges using robust quantitative analysis.
