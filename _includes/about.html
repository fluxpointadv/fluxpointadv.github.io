About

Fluxpoint Advisory is founded by two researchers with PhDs from the University of Tokyo, with experience across academia, industry, and operational environmental systems in Sri Lanka, Japan and the UK.

We work across environmental systems analysis, life cycle assessment, carbon accounting, and climate and ocean modelling, supporting applied research and decision-making in sustainability and Net Zero contexts.

Our experience includes international collaborations across Malaysia, Japan, and the UK, as well as work in academic, industry, and operational climate and environmental settings.
